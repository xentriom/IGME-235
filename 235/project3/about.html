Veggie Slicer

A recreation of a simplistic mobile game called Fruit Ninja. Veggie Slicer takes it to the next level with various upgrades purchasable via the shop as well as three modes for you to play. Unleash your inner ninja and slice through each and every veggie that comes your way. Beware, some veggies may have a negative impact on your overall money!

About Veggie Slicer

Veggie Slicer is a solo project developed in IGME235, Intro to Game Web Tech. The goal was to develop and deploy a game using PIXI or Phaser.

Game Modes

Launching with 3 initial game modes, one for practice and 2 for the score and currency.

Upgrades

With various upgrades to the trail from the shop, players can further customize and improve their gaming experience by microtransactions within the game. Each veggie cut during the game will result in a increase or decrease of currency

Settings

With game customizations such as saving data as well as muting and adjusting game volumn to your preferance! You can also view your stats such as total coins earned, total veggies sliced, and the amount of veggies sliced.

OVERCOMING THE HURDLE

Getting the project started and getting the flow was the hard part. Once i got started, got things layed out, everything else was plug and chug. There was a few issued I had that made me pause and think but nothing that difficult. After getting about 70% of the way done, I did realize a significant flaw and I redid the whole variable system to make use of JavaScript ovbjects and splitting them up into game data, adjustable data, and session data. This change made everything else such as upgrades and setting chages very easy.

WHAT CHANGED?

Many things changed since my first vision of this project. One of the change was a more and better shop which consisted of more upgrades. As I worked, I realize how difficult it was so I made the colors a majority of the upgrade. Another major feature I ended up never adding was the effect of the veggie being split in half after falling, that was too much....
